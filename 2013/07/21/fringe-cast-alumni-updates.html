We are so happy to be putting on Riverview High: The Musical again this year at the Firehall Arts Centre. After our sell out show at the Vancouver Fringe 2012 and winning Pick of the Fringe and the 2013 Outstanding Production Ovation Award, we're super excited to see some of our former cast members in some Vancouver shows.

Daniel Cardoso and Cathy Wilmot are in the cast of Legally Blonde put on by Theatre Under the Stars Vancouver - July 11, 2013 to August 16, 2013.

Georgia Swinton, Lena Dabrusin and Julia Ullrich in How to Succeed In Business Without Really Trying - July 10, 2013 to August 17, 2013.

The 2013 production of Riverview High has been revised with 2 new songs. Not only do we have some of Vancouver's brightest up and coming actors, but some well seasoned actors as well.

Erik Gow *will be taking the stage in the leading role of Alex with *Alex Gullason and Ranae Miller as his love interests Cathy and Erica. All three are extremely talented and definitely not to be missed.

Tickets are starting from $18 and are on sale online at FirehallArtsCentre.ca. Tickets will sell out, so purchase in advance to avoid disappointment.
